2. **Check browser console:** Press F12 â†’ Console tab â†’ Look for errors
3. **Verify script loads:** Check that `elfsightcdn.com/platform.js` loads

1. **Check Elfsight dashboard:** Verify widget is active and connected to Google

3. **Google Business Profile:** Ensure you have published reviews on Google
4. **Refresh connection:** In Elfsight, reconnect to Google Business Profile

3. **Check mobile view:** Widget adapts automatically to screen size

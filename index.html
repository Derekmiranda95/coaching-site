Meet Derek

Your Partner in Transformation

After undergoing a personal lifestyle transformation that inspired those around them, Derek found a passion for helping others lead healthier lives and became a coach four years ago.

As an ACE Certified Personal Trainer and Precision Nutrition Level 1 Coach with 4+ years of experience and 2 professional certifications, Derek has developed expertise through hands-on experience, building a comprehensive knowledge base in corrective exercise, mobility, nutrition, strength, and performance training.

With 100% dedication to his clients, Derek's greatest joy comes from motivating them to embrace the gym experience, push their limits, and have fun along the way.

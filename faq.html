Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about training with FreeBody Performance

Once-weekly sessions don't create meaningful progress. Your coaching focuses on clients who want consistent training and real results.

Research and experience show that training frequency of 2-3 times per week produces significantly better outcomes in terms of strength gains, mobility improvements, and sustainable habit formation.

The Performance membership (2×/week) is our most popular option — it's the best blend of consistency and results.

This frequency allows for adequate recovery between sessions while maintaining enough training stimulus to drive continuous progress. Most clients see significant improvements in strength, mobility, and overall fitness within 8-12 weeks.

The Flex Pack is designed exactly for that: 10 sessions, 3 months to use them.

This option is perfect for clients with unpredictable schedules, frequent travel, or those who want to try our coaching before committing to a monthly membership. You get the same high-quality training without the monthly billing structure.

Membership sessions roll for 30 days, up to 8 total. This banking system gives you flexibility when life gets busy, but encourages consistent training habits. Flex Pack sessions do not bank — they simply expire after 3 months from purchase date.

We require 12 hours minimum notice for all cancellations.

  • Cancel 12+ hours in advance: Your session can be rescheduled to another available time
  • Cancel inside 12 hours: The session is forfeited and deducted from your package

This policy protects the integrity of our schedule and ensures we can offer quality service to all clients. Repeated late cancellations may affect your access to preferred time slots.

Yes, memberships can be paused once every 3 months for up to 2 weeks.

Requirements for pausing:

  • Provide 7 days advance notice
  • No session accumulation during pause period
  • Billing resumes automatically after pause period
  • Banked sessions must be used within 30 days after resuming

Note: Flex Packs cannot be paused — the 3-month expiration continues regardless.

Sick Days:

  • Notify before 12 hours → reschedule or virtual recovery session
  • Notify within 12 hours → session is forfeited
  • Virtual recovery sessions can be arranged if appropriate for your condition

Travel:

  • Provide 7 days notice to bank that week's sessions (up to the 8-session cap)
  • Virtual sessions available anywhere with internet connection
  • Billing does not pause for travel alone
  • Consider switching to virtual sessions during travel periods

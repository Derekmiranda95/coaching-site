Frequently Asked Questions

How does online programming work?

After signing up, you'll complete an intake form covering your goals, experience, equipment, and schedule. Derek will design your custom program and deliver it through a mobile app where you can track workouts, log progress, and message directly with your coach.

What equipment do I need?

Programs can be designed for any setup - from full commercial gyms to minimal home equipment. During intake, you'll specify what you have access to, and your program will be customized accordingly.

How often do programs update?

Your program is updated monthly based on your progress, feedback, and goals. You'll have ongoing communication with Derek to ensure the programming stays aligned with your needs.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Online programming is month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime with no penalties or fees.

How is this different from in-person training?

Online programming gives you expert guidance at a more accessible price point, with the flexibility to train on your schedule. You won't have real-time coaching during workouts, but you'll get form check videos, messaging support, and monthly check-ins.

Is nutrition coaching included?

Yes! Your program includes personalized nutrition guidance with macro targets, meal planning strategies, and ongoing nutrition coaching to support your goals.
